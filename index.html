Note: KarpeAssist does not handle financial, tax, bookkeeping, or health records. All spreadsheet and document work is limited to general administrative or organizational data.

How it works

  1. Email a task. Include any files/links and the format you want back.
  2. Evening confirm. Tasks sent by 7 PM ET get a reply (Yes/No) by 8 PM ET.
  3. Delivery. Small items are often delivered by late evening or overnight; larger ones get the next available evening.

Rates

$30 per hour. 1 hour minimum. All sessions are billed in full hours, rounded up to the next full hour. Most small tasks finish within the first hour. For larger items, I confirm scope and get approval before any additional billing.

FAQs

When will I hear back after sending a task?

Sunday–Thursday, I confirm all new requests in the evening. Tasks sent by 7 PM ET get a reply (Yes/No) by 8 PM ET.

Can I send any size task and expect it back the same evening?

Smaller requests are often completed by late evening or overnight. Larger items may need more time. I always confirm scope and give you a delivery estimate before I begin.

How do billing and rates work?

$30/hr remote. 1 hour minimum. All sessions are billed in full hours, rounded up to the next full hour.

Stripe invoice preferred; PayPal available.

What if you are unavailable?

I do not take tasks on Friday or Saturday. If I cannot accept a task on a Sunday through Thursday evening, I will confirm the next available evening during my reply.

Do you take meetings or calls?

No calls or meetings. I am email first so you can fire off tasks quickly and get results without scheduling.

Is this remote only?

Yes, remote only. No in-person errands or printer setup.
